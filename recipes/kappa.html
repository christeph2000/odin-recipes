Kappa

A healthy and delicious Tapioca (sabudana) recipe to cook at home! Boiled tapiocas are cooked in a ground paste of coconut and spices, tempered with sauteed onions, red cillies, mustard seeds and curry leaves.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tapioca (cubed)
  • Little Salt
  • 1 Coconut
  • 1 Cumin seeds
  • 1-2 Green chilli

Steps

  1. Peel off the skin from the tapiocas, cut into cubes, wash under running water.
  2. Boil the tapiocas along with water and little salt until it turns tender and well cooked.
  3. In a large saucepan, add the cooked tapiocas and the ground paste.
  4. Cook for about 5-10 minutes, until the gravy starts to thicken and is combined well.
  5. Heat a small pan over medium heat, add oil.
  6. Add the chopped small onions and saute for a few minutes.
  7. Add dry red chilies and curry leaves, saute till onions have turned brown.
  8. Pour this tempering onto the cooked tapioca.
  9. Keep the tapioca covered for sometime so that the flavors from the tempering will get into the dish.
